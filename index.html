Emmett J. Conrad High School.

Emmett J. Conrad High School is a public high school located in Vickery Meadow, Dallas, Texas.

It covers grades 9-12, is a part of the Dallas Independent School District.

Emmett J. Conrad High School is the only comprehensive high school in the state of Texas to earn the distinguished level for all 5 National Academy Foundation Academies.

There are various academy at Conrad.

Such as, Academy of Engineering, Academy of Finance, Academy of Health and Science, Academy of Hospitality and Tourism, and Academy of Information Technology.

NAF students working in our academies graduate with a diploma and many other certifications.

Conrad also help with preparation for College and Work.

It provide Summer and School Year Internships as well.

There are students from different countries.

Conrad doesn't tolerate any discrimination against race at all.
